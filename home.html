About Us..

Auto-Mart is a leading car detailing company in Sri Lanka with over 10 years of experience in the industry. We have branches in Colombo, Galle, Kandy, and Kurunegala.
Cleaning and restoring a car to like-new condition is the art and craft of professional automotive detailing. A car wash is far less precise and labor-intensive than car detailing services. Typically, an automobile passes through an automated system at a car wash to get the exterior cleaned.
